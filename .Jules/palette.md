
## 2026-06-03 - RFQ Modal Micro-UX & Accessibility
**Learning:** Adding a character counter and proper label associations in static HTML significantly improves form usability and screen reader support without needing a heavy framework. Removing accidental hover triggers prevents intrusive UI behavior.
**Action:** Always check for accidental onmouseenter on high-intent buttons and ensure all form fields have semantic labels and aria-required for mandatory inputs.
