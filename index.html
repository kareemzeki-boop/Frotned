Common Questions
The platform is new — how do I know anyone will see my listing?
That is exactly why we are approaching you now rather than in six months. Platforms that take off in B2B construction do so because a credible supplier foundation was established early. Your listing will be seen by every architect who uses CladWise UAE from day one — and that audience grows over time. The alternative is joining after your competitors do.
We already appear on other directories.
General UAE business directories list every type of contractor. CladWise UAE lists only façade material suppliers — and only verified ones. Architects using this platform are specifically searching for façade systems, compliance documentation, and technical data. That is a fundamentally different audience to a general directory.
What does the free founding listing include?
The full platform experience, with no time limit on the listing itself. Build your company profile, upload your compliance documents, list your materials and services, and add your notable projects. Your listing goes live and remains active as long as CladWise UAE operates. Suppliers who want verification, priority placement, and RFQ routing can upgrade to Verified Supplier at AED 999/year. The Featured plan at AED 2,499/year adds top-category placement and dedicated support. The base listing stays free permanently.
How long does verification take?
Standard verification is completed within 2 business days of document submission. Founding Supplier and Featured applications are prioritised within 48 hours. You will receive direct email confirmation when your verified badge is issued.
